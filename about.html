About Me

I am honest, dedicated, relentless, and patient. Currently a junior at Westwood High School, I balance rigorous academics, competitive tennis, and meaningful volunteering.

Beyond the classroom and the court, I enjoy reading fiction, solving Rubik’s cubes, and practicing taekwondo. My curiosity for problem-solving extends to technology as well—I began coding with robotics in elementary school and have since grown into programming with Python and Raspberry Pi.

Looking forward, I aim to continue excelling in tennis and academics while pursuing opportunities that challenge me to learn, grow, and give back to my community.
