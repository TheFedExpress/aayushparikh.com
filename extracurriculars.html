Athletics & Discipline

Taekwondo

I earned my black belt in taekwondo, a discipline that taught me focus, control, and respect. The training instilled in me the importance of mental strength and self-discipline—qualities that carry over into everything I do.

Track & Field

In both 7th and 8th grade, I competed for my middle school track team at the district level. I was the fastest runner in my school for both the 1600m and 2400m events, pushing myself to maintain endurance and speed over distance.

Hobbies & Interests

Outdoor Adventures

I love hiking and camping, especially challenging trips like summiting Angel's Landing in Zion National Park or camping in freezing temperatures with my dad. These trips test both physical endurance and mental resilience.

Mental Puzzles & Music

At home, I unwind by timing myself on the Rubik's cube, which I can solve in under thirty seconds. I also have played in the Orchestra for 4 years. These hobbies remind me that focus can turn into progress in almost anything.
