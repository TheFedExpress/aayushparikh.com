Giving back through tennis has been one of the most meaningful parts of my life. I'm the director of the Austin branch of First Racquet, a nonprofit originally founded in Houston. Our goal is to make tennis more accessible for kids who wouldn't otherwise have the chance to play.

We organize tournaments and collect donations—rackets, shoes, clothing, strings, and balls—which we send to underprivileged children in Angola. Through this work, I've learned that leadership isn't about recognition; it's about consistency. Whether I'm coordinating volunteers or sorting gear, I'm reminded that small actions can have a big impact when done with purpose.
