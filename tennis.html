Freshman Year

Joined the Round Rock High School varsity team as the youngest player, competing in singles. Advanced through the early rounds of the solo season before losing in the second round of Districts.

Sophomore Year

Transferred to Westwood High School, a program known for its intensity and discipline. In the team season, we went on to win the state championship. In the solo season, I competed in singles and advanced to the second round of Regionals.

Junior Year

Named team captain. In the team season, we fell just short of repeating as champions, finishing as state runner-up. In the solo season, I competed in doubles and qualified for state. Leadership is less about giving instructions and more about setting an example through composure, integrity, and effort.
