Freshman Year

I joined the Round Rock High School varsity team as the youngest player, competing in singles. I advanced through the early rounds of the solo season before losing in the second round of Districts.

Sophomore Year

I transferred to Westwood High School, a program known for its intensity and discipline. In the team season, we went on to win the state championship. In the solo season, I competed in singles and advanced to the second round of Regionals.

Junior Year

I was named team captain. In the team season, we fell just short of repeating as champions, finishing as state runner-up. In the solo season, I competed in doubles and qualified for state, a milestone I'm proud of. Leadership, I've found, is less about giving instructions and more about setting an example through composure, integrity, and effort.
